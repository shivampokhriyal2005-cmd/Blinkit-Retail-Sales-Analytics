#  Blinkit Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard

##  Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** developed to analyze Blinkit's retail sales performance. The dashboard provides insights into sales trends, product categories, outlet performance, customer ratings, and inventory distribution, enabling data-driven business decisions.



##  Objectives

* Analyze overall retail sales performance.
* Identify top-performing product categories.
* Compare sales across different outlet tiers and sizes.
* Understand customer preferences based on fat content.
* Visualize key business KPIs for quick decision-making.



##  Dashboard Features

*  Total Sales KPI
*  Average Sales
*  Number of Items Sold
*  Average Customer Rating
*  Fat Content Analysis
*  Sales by Item Type
*  Sales by Outlet Tier
*  Sales by Outlet Location
*  Outlet Size Analysis
*  Outlet Establishment Trend
*  Business Insights Panel



##  Key Insights

* Tier 3 outlets generated the highest sales.
* Low Fat products contributed **64.6%** of total sales.
* Fruits & Vegetables emerged as the highest-selling product category.
* Medium-sized outlets generated the highest revenue.
* The average customer rating across all products is **3.92/5**.
